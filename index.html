Submitting Assignments

To submit assignments, you must email me a zipped folder named LASTNAME_FIRSTNAME_HW# containing your .cpp files.

Each .cpp file must be named LASTNAME_FIRSTNAME_QUESTION#

The subject line of your email must be LASTNAME FIRSTNAME HW#

Each question of the assignment must be completed as a standalone .cpp file.

For example, suppose I am submitting assignment 2, which contains 3 questions.

I would email a zipped folder named KACZMARCZYK_JAMES_HW2 containing three files:

  • KACZMARCZYK_JAMES_1.cpp
  • KACZMARCZYK_JAMES_2.cpp
  • KACZMARCZYK_JAMES_3.cpp

The subject line of my email would be KACZMARCZYK JAMES 2
